Philosophy

Cooking, kept honest.

Every dish on our table begins the way it has since the day we opened — vegetables cut by hand, proteins marinated overnight, sauces reduced rather than thickened. We've never believed abundance and care are opposites. Forty dishes does not mean forty shortcuts.

It means a kitchen busy enough, for long enough, to get every one of them right.
