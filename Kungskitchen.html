Our Philosophy

Cooking is an act
of generosity

At Kung's Kitchen, every dish that arrives at the buffet table represents hours of careful preparation — sauces reduced low and slow, vegetables cut to order, proteins marinated overnight. We believe in feeding people well, not feeding them just enough.

From the aromatic Thai curries to the delicate Japanese rolls, each station is a window into a distinct culinary tradition — and we guard those traditions with respect.

The Buffet

A world of flavour,
under one roof

Our rotating selection spans four Asian cuisines — every visit brings something new. Below is a taste of what awaits.

🥢
Chinese Classics

Wok-fried rice, Peking-style duck, spring rolls, dim sum, honey pork ribs and our signature sweet-sour glazes.

🌿
Thai Selections

Green and red curries, pad thai, tom kha soup, basil chicken and fresh papaya salad with tamarind dressing.

🍱
Japanese Station

Maki rolls, edamame, teriyaki glazes, miso soup and tempura — light, precise and perfectly balanced.

🔥
Hot Dishes

Rotating daily specials — braised beef, seafood wok, spicy tofu and seasonal vegetables in rich house-made sauces.

🥗
Fresh & Light

Salad bar with Asian dressings, fresh fruit, pickled vegetables and cleansing broth soups.

🍮
Desserts

Mango pudding, lychee jelly, sesame balls and seasonal sweets to close your meal on a gentle note.
